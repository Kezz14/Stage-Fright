About

Stage Fright in an electrifying rock bank known for their high-energy performances and unforgettable sound. With their roots in the vibrant music scenes of New York, the band’s sound is an evocative blend of poetic vulnerability, gritty energy, and intricate musicality. Stage Fright is more than a band—it’s a testament to the power of music to transform pain into art, chaos into harmony, and isolation into connection. With each track and every performance, they invite listeners to feel deeply, live boldly, and find solace in the shared human experience.

The Band Members

Alex Turner

Lead Vocal

Alex Turner is a shy yet charismatic figure. He has a distinct, almost haunting voice that could go from fragile and delicate to fiery and intense.

Jamie Smith

Guitarist

Jamie Smith discovered his father's guitar hidden in a closet at the age of 12. He gained a distinct ability to make his guitar sing, capturing emotions in his playing that words couldn’t express.

Taylor Green

Bassist

Taylor Green is the quiet force that anchors the band's sound. He provides the groove and the grounding with his calm but confident bass sound.

Morgan Lee

Drummer

Morgan Lee is the rhythmic heartbeat of the band. When he was 14, he picked up his first drum kit. To him, it wasn’t about perfection; it was about feeling the rhythm, the power, and the release of the drums.
